We study how governance design, staking incentives, and liquidity mining policies alter the cross-sectional pricing of platform tokens. The project combines blockchain transaction graphs, centralized exchange order books, and DeFi protocol data to trace where shocks originate and how they propagate to equities and fixed-income products.
